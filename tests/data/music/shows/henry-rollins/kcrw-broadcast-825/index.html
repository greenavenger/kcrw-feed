KCRW Broadcast 825

Jan. 25, 2025 Music

Fanatic! I hope you had a great week and had a chance to listen to some music.

You might have noticed we’ve been listening to a band called Horsegirl for the last few weeks. Their album Versions Of Modern Performance is really good. Thanks to Fanatic Denise for the heads up. Yesterday I was told the band has a new album slated for February. I went to Bandcamp and found this to be true. The preorder tracks sound really good. We’ll be getting into the album as soon as possible. Here’s the info in case you’re interested.

https://thisishorsegirl.bandcamp.com/album/phonetics-on-and-on

Also, those who preordered the new Dax Riggs record 7 Songs For Spiders might have noticed the tracks became available yesterday for download. Hopefully, the vinyl is in the mail soon.

https://daxriggs.bandcamp.com/album/7-songs-for-spiders

 Fanatic McGinty gave me the heads up on a cool band from Brighton, England called Lambrini Girls and their album Who Let The Dogs Out. On their Bandcamp page, the heading reads, “party music for gay angry sluts.” Hooray! We’ll start tracks soon.

https://lambrinigirlsband.bandcamp.com/album/who-let-the-dogs-out

 We have what you will hopefully find to be a tremendous batch of tunes specifically selected and ordered for your maximum enjoyment. Engineer X and I have been putting the finishing touches on next week’s show. I’ll be back here to give you those details next Saturday.

Until then, keep the music playing and STAY FANATIC!!! 

–– Henry
